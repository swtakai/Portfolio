Hunter

I competed in the Children's Pony Hunters as well as the Pony Hunters with my pony Billy. We won many championships across the midwest, and competed at the USEF Pony Finals in 2015. At pony finals, we placed 23rd out of 86 in the Large Green Pony Hunter Over Fences.

I competed in the Children's Hunters as well as the Junior Hunters with my Luna. We competed at AA shows across Michigan, and competed at the USEF Junior Hunter Finals in 2017. We placed in 2nd on the flat.

Equitation

I competed with Billy in the Pony Equitation. We won the Hunter Jumper Association of Michigan Pony Medal Final in 2016.

I competed in the age equitation and the big equitation with Luc. We won the Maclay at Waterloo Hunt Club as well as the NCEA medal at the World Equestrian Center. We qualified for and competed in Maclay Regionals, NCEA Finals, HJAM Medal Finals, and NHS Medal Finals.

Jumper

This summer I competed with Mike in jumper classes. We got champion at the Great Lakes Equestrian Festival as well as multiple blue ribbons throughout the summer.

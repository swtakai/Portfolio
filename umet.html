The Michigan Equestrian Team accepts all levels of riders. We have competing and non-competing members. Our competition team competes in Zone 6 Region 4 of the Intercollegiate Horse Show Association (IHSA).

I have been a member of the Michigan Equestrian Team since freshman year. In my sophomore year, I served as the Social Coordinator. This year I am the Vice President as well as the Social Media Coordinator. Next year I will be the President.
